<p>Nairobi 30th June 2014…Kenyatta University (KU), today launched its new KU Digital School in a move that is set to address the increased demand for improved higher education e-Learning programs in Kenya.</p>
<p>The launch of KU Digital School demonstrates the University’s understanding of the unique benefits and advantages of digital technology in the education sector. At a time when change is faster than ever, a key advantage of e-Learning - the use of technology to deliver training anytime, anyplace - is that it has faster delivery cycle times than traditional classroom-based instruction.&nbsp;</p>
